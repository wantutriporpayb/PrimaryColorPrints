ABOUT US

PrimaryColor Prints Inc. (PCPI) is a print service company dedicated to providing innovative solutions to our customers' prepress and printing requirements.

Founded on June 14th, 2009, PCPI began as a Prepress company servicing offset print houses in Makati, which included desktop publishing, color matching, color proofing, imposition of artworks for press, and other prepress activities.

As PCPI's customer base grew and the demand for low-volume prints increased, the company expanded its services and ventured into new markets. In April 2012, PCPI acquired a new state-of-the-art digital press, enabling the development of new print products and offering them to small businesses and corporate clients.

From then on, the company has further diversified its portfolio by entering the large-format digital printing industry, acquiring a high- resolution large-format printer. This expansion provides our existing customers with an even wider range of solutions and creates new opportunities to enter different market segments.

From its beginnings as a prepress service provider to its current status as a digital advertising print shop, PCPI has continued to evolve and grow. Our unwavering commitment to customer success has always been our top priority and has been instrumental in driving our growth and success.
